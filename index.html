"DRONE DOME" CONFERENCE


The purpose of the event is to strengthen the protection of cities and strategic facilities in Finland from potential drone attacks from the Russian Federation and to develop the defense drone industry in Finland.


Finland, Tampere 27/09/2025-29/09/2025


Before the start of the Conference:
Buy tickets
The experience of the Ukrainian-Russian war has shown that 85% of combat damage to military personnel, equipment, buildings and civilians is caused by Drones. Drones are switching to AI, forming into Swarms. The next step is the creation of SuperSystems that automatically control the Drone system for protecting cities - we called this Project "Drone Dome". Our task is to gather the best drone defenses of Ukraine, together with Finland, into the "Drone Dome" and deploy them for the systemic protection of our cities.

The Drone Dome Conference is attended by 70 participants:

Ukrainian Manufacturers:
Drones, software, electronic warfare, electronic warfare systems, surveillance stations and others that form the basis for the "Drone Dome" and that have passed preliminary selection at military training grounds near Kharkiv in combat conditions - 10 participants
Ukrainian military
who worked with these products in combat conditions, are ready to share their experience of use and application tactics with European colleagues - 10 participants
European Drone Manufacturers
who seek to create Joint Ventures with Ukrainian Manufacturers - 20 participants
Finnish Territorial Defence Forces
and invited Military and Government officials of Finland - 20 participants
Investment Funds of Europe
who seek to invest in cutting-edge innovations in Europe and involve the Projects they have already invested in into the "Drone Dome" system - 10 participants
